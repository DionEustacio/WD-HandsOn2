Orange Cat

also known as ginger or marmalade cats, have fur that ranges from light golden to deep reddish-orange. Their coat can be solid orange or have tabby patterns with stripes or swirls. They often have green or gold eyes. Orange cats are usually male due to genetics. They're known for friendly, outgoing personalities and are popular in folklore and media.
